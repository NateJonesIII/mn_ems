Frequently Asked Questions

To create an event, go to your profile dashboard and click on the "Create Event" button. Fill out the required information such as event name, date, location, and description. Once submitted, your event will be listed on the platform.

You can edit your event details by visiting your event's page from your dashboard. Click on the "Edit" button next to your event and update the necessary information such as date, time, or description. Remember to save your changes.

To delete an event, navigate to your event's page from your dashboard. Click on the "Delete" button, confirm your decision, and your event will be removed from the platform. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

You can view events you're attending by going to your profile page and selecting the "Events Attending" tab. Here, you'll find a list of events you've RSVP'd to or registered for.
